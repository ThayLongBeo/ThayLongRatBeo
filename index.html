Achievements

During his time at Real Madrid from 2009 to 2018, Cristiano Ronaldo became the club's all-time leading goalscorer and played a pivotal role in their success. He secured four UEFA Champions League titles, two La Liga championships, and set records for individual achievements, earning multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or awards. More information

Cristiano Ronaldo's tenure at Real Madrid was marked by prolific goal-scoring, establishing him as the all-time leading goal scorer for the club. He achieved numerous personal records, including multiple hat-tricks, showcasing his exceptional skill and impact on the team.

His contributions were pivotal in Real Madrid's success in the UEFA Champions League, playing a crucial role in securing the title during the memorable 2017-2018 campaign where he scored an impressive 15 goals. Ronaldo's remarkable performances in Europe solidified his status as one of the greatest players in the club's history.

Beyond the statistics, Ronaldo's playing style, characterized by speed and powerful shots, became iconic for Real Madrid, creating a lasting connection with the fans. His legacy extends beyond the numerical achievements, symbolizing an era of unparalleled success and leaving an enduring influence on the rich history of the club.
